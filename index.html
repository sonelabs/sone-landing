Our Story

About SONE

SONE began with a simple vision: to give patients greater autonomy. We achieve this through technology that understands human intention via the most natural interface, our eyes.

Patients with communication difficulties are three times more likely to experience adverse events in hospitals, including falls, when compared to patients without communication-related impairments. Our enhanced call system makes caregiver-patient communication more seamless and effective, providing an alternative solution when traditional methods fail.

With SONE, healthcare facilities can reduce response times, increase patient safety, and ultimately improve the quality of care.
